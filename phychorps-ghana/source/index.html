All Ghanaians should be treated with dignity and without stigmatization

We believe every individual no matter their status deserves fair and optimal mental health services

We are motivated by the fact that the principles and methods of psychological care can ease the pain of mental illness and that psychology graduates armed with these can make a big difference to the lives of individuals, families and communities, and change the way health care is delivered in Ghana.

Our Projects

Bringing Psychology into communities

Recruiting Students for Psych Corps Program

Students are recruited from final year Psychology programmes of public universities who go on to do National Service after training in Psychology First Aid.

Click to Register

Training and Orientation

We give students an orientation in Psychological First Aid, Ethics of working in communities and How to support people with serious mental illness. We also give some coaching for psycho-education.

Read More

Posting and Placement

After the training, the students are ready to be posted by the National Service Scheme (NSS) to the various hospitals.
