What You Need To Know About Mental Health in Ghana

Bridging the gap in mental health care treatment, through psych education and more.

Learn More

All Ghanaians should be treated with dignity and without stigmatization

We believe every individual no matter their status deserves fair and optimal mental health services

We are motivated by the fact that the principles and methods of psychological care can ease the pain of mental illness and that psychology graduates armed with these can make a big difference to the lives of individuals, families and communities, and change the way health care is delivered in Ghana.
